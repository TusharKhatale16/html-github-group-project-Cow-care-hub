Welcome to CowCare Hub

CowCare Hub is a complete informational student project created using HTML. It provides knowledge about cow care, indigenous cow breeds, common cow diseases, and the importance of cows in Indian agriculture and culture.

Our mission is to spread awareness about animal welfare, ethical cow care and sustainable farming practices.

Common Cow Diseases

Foot and Mouth Disease is a highly contagious viral disease affecting cows. It spreads rapidly and reduces milk production.

  • Foot and Mouth Disease
  • Mastitis
  • Milk Fever
  • Skin Infections

Early detection and proper treatment can save cows. Cows can suffer from various diseases due to poor hygiene, lack of nutrition and improper medical care. Early identification and timely treatment can save cows and reduce losses.
